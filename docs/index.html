Hi! Thanks for checking out my website.

I’m Daniël Berghorst, going online by Basketbird(y), and I’m currently in the second year of studying game development at the HKU to get better at realising my vision, artistically, practically and professionally.

My aim is to create games that are not only enjoyable, but also spark a sense of curiosity to find out more, whether that is through appealing art, engaging narrative, fun gameplay or a combination of all.

Even though I enjoy a broad range of things, my true passion lies in experiences that manage to integrate the awesome and mysterious elements of fantasy in a realistic and believable way. My fascination with natural history and sci-fi fosters a curiosity towards the way unknown elements would theoretically integrate into any fictional or real world.
